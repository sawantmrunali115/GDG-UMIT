About Us

Building the Future of

Women in Tech

GDG On Campus UMIT is a student-led community supported by Google Developers Groups that fosters learning, collaboration, and innovation in technical areas among university students.

We focus on empowering women in tech through hands-on learning, workshops, projects, and collaboration. We create opportunities for students to explore modern technologies, build real-world skills, and grow together as confident, innovative developers.
